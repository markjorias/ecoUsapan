About the Service

This service allows individuals and community groups to view available seedlings and submit digital requests for tree-growing and environmental activities such as reforestation, mangrove planting, and community greening.

The request process is fully digital through this app to make seedling access easier and more organized.

However, approved seedlings must be personally claimed at a designated location, such as a DENR office, DENR-accredited nursery, or an assigned barangay distribution point, on the scheduled pickup date.

Who may request

This service is open to individuals and community groups who wish to contribute to environmental protection and greening initiatives.

Eligible requestors include:

  • Individual environmentalists and volunteers engaged in tree growing or habitat restoration
  • Community members organizing small-scale greening or reforestation activities
  • Youth groups, student organizations, and school-based eco-clubs
  • Homeowners or neighborhood groups conducting community tree-planting projects
  • Informal environmental groups and advocacy collectives

Requests should be non-commercial, community-oriented, and intended for environmental conservation, restoration, or public benefit.
