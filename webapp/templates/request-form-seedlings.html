Request Form

Step 1: Select Seedlings

Select the seedlings you need and specify the quantity.

Step 2: Requester Info

Leave blank if applying as an individual.

Step 3: Details & Plan

Briefly explain why and how you will plant the seedlings. No formal proposal needed.

Step 4: Uploads & Confirm

Please upload at least one clear photo to help verify and support your request.

Required files:
• Photo of the planting site (land, backyard, roadside, coastal area, etc.)
• Photo of requester or group at the site (optional if uncomfortable, but recommended)
Tap to upload JPG / PNG

Note: These photos will only be used to confirm suitability of the request and will not be shared publicly.

All requests submitted through this app are subject to a due process and verification.

Submission of this digital form does not guarantee automatic approval.

To ensure fair and proper distribution, additional endorsement or proof (such as a barangay note, community confirmation, or site verification) may be required before a request is approved.

You will be notified through the app if additional documents or validation steps are needed.

Thank you for helping us keep this program transparent, accountable, and community-focused.
